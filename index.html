AFTER LISTENING
Task 1:Discuss the questions below.
1. What message do you think the song is trying to convey?
2. Have you ever felt like the person described in the song, trying to meet certain beauty standards?
3. What do you think the singer means by “There’s a hope that’s waiting for you in the dark”?
4. What impact do you think songs like this can have on people's self-esteem and body image?

Task 2. Reflect on your own experiences with self-image and beauty standards. Jot down your thoughts in your copybook. Think about a time when you felt pressured to conform to certain beauty standards and how you handled it.
